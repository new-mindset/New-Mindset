New Mindset!

A DigiInventors 2023-24 Submission! :D



The Concept

New Mindset would be a web-based platform where
you and another user would be
put into a chatroom. This
'other user' would have simillar
problems in life to yours.
Being able to find someone
who relates to your problems can be
rather soothing and makes you feel
way better when you realize you
aren't alone.
